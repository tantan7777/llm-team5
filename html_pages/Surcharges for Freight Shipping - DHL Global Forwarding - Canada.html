Updated: December 13, 2010

International Security Surcharges

Effective December 18, 2006, an Indirect Carrier Security Fee (ISF) of US$5.00 will be implemented on all international air export cargo as a result of an Emergency Amendment recently issued by the TSA effective November 27, 2006.

 
Effective: December 18, 2006
